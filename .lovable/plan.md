

## Wild Labs — Premium Landing Page

A cinematic, dark, single-page website for an independent game studio. The page feels like entering a secret experimental facility.

### Brand Setup
- Pure black (#000000) background with gold (#F4C542) and electric cyan (#19E0D4) accents
- Bold geometric sans-serif (Inter/Space Grotesk) for headlines, clean sans-serif for body
- Wolf logo assets embedded throughout (hero, about section, footer)
- Subtle glow effects, high contrast, large spacing

### Page Sections

**1. Hero (Full Screen)**
- Animated particle/grid background on black
- Large wolf logo centered with subtle glow
- "WILD LABS" headline, cinematic subline
- "Enter the Lab" button with cyan glow hover effect
- Faint scanline overlay for atmosphere

**2. About the Studio (Two Column)**
- Left: Large glowing wolf emblem with cyan glow aura
- Right: "A studio built for experiments" headline + body text
- Subtle fade-in on scroll

**3. Philosophy (Three Cards)**
- Experimentation, Player Obsession, Small Teams
- Each card with minimal icon, cyan accent border/glow
- Fade-in animation on scroll entry

**4. In the Lab (Atmospheric)**
- Blueprint grid background with technical diagram lines
- "In the Lab" headline with teaser text
- UI-style overlay elements for research lab feel

**5. Join Us (Minimal)**
- Gold accent section
- "Work With Us" headline + "Contact" button with gold glow

**6. Footer**
- Small wolf icon centered
- Wild Labs • Stockholm • hello@wildlabs.se
- Minimal black design

### Interactions & Effects
- CSS particle animation on hero background
- Intersection Observer fade-in animations for sections
- Subtle scanline CSS overlay
- Button glow effects on hover (cyan for primary, gold for contact)
- Smooth scroll navigation from "Enter the Lab" button
